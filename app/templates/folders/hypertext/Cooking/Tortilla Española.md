# Tortilla de patatas

**Ingredients**
- 500 g potatoes, peeled and thinly sliced
- 1 onion, thinly sliced
- 6 eggs
- 100 ml olive oil
- Salt

**Method**
1. Heat oil in a non-stick pan. Add potatoes and onions; cook gently, stirring, until tender but not browned (about 20 minutes).
2. Drain and cool slightly. Beat eggs in a bowl, season with salt, and combine with the drained mixture.
3. Return to the pan with a spoon of oil. Cook slowly, lifting the edges as it sets.
4. Invert onto a plate, then slide back into the pan to cook the other side.
5. Rest before slicing. Serve in wedges.
