# Confit de canard

**Ingredients**
- 4 duck legs with thighs
- 1 tbsp salt
- 1 tsp black pepper
- 4 sprigs thyme
- 3 cloves garlic, smashed
- Duck fat (enough to submerge legs)

**Method**
1. Rub duck legs with salt, pepper, thyme, and garlic. Refrigerate overnight.
2. Rinse, pat dry, and place in an ovenproof pot. Cover fully with duck fat.
3. Bake at 120°C (250°F) for 2.5–3 hours until tender.
4. Cool in fat. Reheat skin-side-down in a skillet to crisp before serving.
5. Serve with lentils or bitter greens.
